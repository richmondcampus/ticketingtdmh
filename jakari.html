FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I book a package?
You can book online by sending us an inquiry through this website's form or live chat. You can also visit us in person at SM Center Angono, Rizal. Our team will respond within 1 hour during business hours.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept GCash, Maya, bank transfer (BDO, BPI, Metrobank), credit/debit card via online payment link, and cash payment at our branch inside SM Center Angono.
Can I customize a travel package?
Absolutely! We specialize in tailor-made packages for barkadas, families, couples, and corporate groups. Just send us your preferred destination, dates, number of travelers, and budget through the inquiry form.
Do you assist with visa applications?
Yes, we provide visa application guidance and document preparation assistance for international destinations. Please inquire directly so our team can advise based on your specific destination.
Is Jakari Travel and Tours accredited?
Yes! Jakari Travel and Tours is an authorized Philippine Explorer partner operating at SM Center Angono, Rizal. We operate with full compliance with DOT regulations.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 30+ days before travel are eligible for a full refund or rebooking. Cancellations within 15–29 days incur a 25% fee. Within 14 days, a 50% fee applies. Please read our full terms or inquire for specific package policies.
