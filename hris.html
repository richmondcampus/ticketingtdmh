Holiday Pay Rules Reference (DOLE)
🔴 Regular Holiday
Not worked: 100% of daily rate
Worked (1st 8hrs): 200% of daily rate
Worked OT: +30% of hourly rate × 1.25
Rest Day + Holiday: 260% of daily rate
🟡 Special Non-Working
Not worked: No pay (no work, no pay)
Worked (1st 8hrs): 130% of daily rate
Worked OT: +30% of hourly rate × 1.25
Rest Day + Special: 150% of daily rate
🟣 Proclamation Holiday
Type depends on proclamation
Usually declared as:
• Regular Holiday, OR
• Special Non-Working Day
Check official gazette for exact type
